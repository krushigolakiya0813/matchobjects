Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

🧸 MatchUp — Kids Learning Game ("we", "our", "the app") is made for children and their families. We deeply respect your child’s privacy. This policy explains, in plain language, what we do — and do not — do with information.

🙅 Information We Do Not Collect

We designed MatchUp to be a safe, private playground. The app does not ask for or collect:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers or birthdays
  • Photos, contacts, location or microphone access
  • Account sign-ups, social logins or passwords
  • Any information that can personally identify your child

📦 Information Stored On the Device

To make the game work and remember progress, some data is saved only on your device (never sent to us):

  • Game progress, best times and unlocked categories
  • Settings such as sound, theme colour and voice preferences

This information stays on the device and is removed when you delete the app.

📢 Advertising

MatchUp is free and supported by advertising delivered through Google AdMob. We may show banner, interstitial (full-screen) and rewarded video ads. Because MatchUp is made for children, we configure AdMob to serve only child-directed, non-personalized ads, in line with Google’s Families Policy, Apple’s App Store guidelines and COPPA.

To deliver these ads, Google AdMob may process limited, non-personal device information (such as a device identifier and approximate region). It does not personally identify your child. You can read Google’s privacy practices at https://policies.google.com/privacy and details about ads for families at support.google.com/admob.

📊 Analytics

To understand how the app is used and to keep improving it, we use Google Firebase (Firebase Analytics and Remote Config) for anonymous, aggregated insights — for example, how many people open the app or which screens are popular. This may include non-personal data such as app opens, device model and app version, and is never used to identify an individual child.

Learn more in Google’s Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy and Firebase’s privacy information at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

👶 Children’s Privacy (COPPA & GDPR-K)

MatchUp is intended for children with the involvement of a parent or guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

🔗 Third-Party Links

Buttons such as “Rate Us”, “More Apps” or “Share” may open the App Store, Google Play or your device’s share sheet. Those services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
